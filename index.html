Biography & Education

Dr. Ikram Ullah completed BS (Hons.) Biotechnology (Gold Medalist) 2008, M.Phil. Biotechnology 2011, and Ph.D. Biotechnology 2018 from Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad. His Ph.D. research focused on synthesis of multifunctional metal nanoparticles for biomedical applications. He has industry experience (R&D manager), international research experience (Turkey), and has supervised BS/MS/PhD students on nanoparticle synthesis, endophytes isolation, molecular identification, metabolomics, and biological screening.

Research Interests

Nanobiotechnology & biomaterials, green synthesis of metal nanoparticles for biomedical applications, highly anticancer medicinal plants and their endophytes, discovery of novel antimicrobial and anticancer metabolites, metabolomics profiling, and nanomedicine development.
